The Story of Aeenak

From Haldwani, Nawaz turned curiosity and hard work into a growing company. What started as small service experiments slowly evolved into Aeenak, a LinkedIn-focused business.

Before Aeenak, we built 2 agencies: One Social and one design.

Together, these agencies helped 100+ businesses grow on social media and build their design language from scratch.

Today, we are focused on something more specific.

We build LinkedIn systems for industry leaders, CXOs, and business owners who want to use LinkedIn the way it was actually designed.

Not as a social media platform.
But as a networking platform.

Because networking platforms are where serious business happens.

Deals between $40K and $120K are rarely closed through entertainment content. They are closed through trust, positioning, and relationships.

To make that happen, we built a full stack of tools, systems, services, and frameworks designed specifically to help industry leaders turn LinkedIn into a high-value deal flow engine.

We started building Aeenak at a time when ChatGPT made everyone believe posting on LinkedIn would become easy.

And in many ways, it did.

Today, almost everyone can write LinkedIn posts. But writing was never the real problem.

The real problem is communication.
Can your message create authority?
Can it attract serious buyers?
Can it position you as someone worth doing business with?

That is the problem we chose to solve.

By questioning the norms,
thinking from first principles,
and staying grounded in real business outcomes.

Now, Team Aeenak has 2 Tools and 4 services that help our clients generate inbound from serious buyers.

With a simple belief: Powerful people decide how they are seen.

Mission

Aeenak helps founders, CXOs, and business owners Position themselves as category authorities, Build high-trust professional networks, Attract serious inbound and Convert conversations into high-value deals.
